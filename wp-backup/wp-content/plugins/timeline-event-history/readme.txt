== Description ==

Timeline is gutenberg ready plugin is useful for show history of organization and as well as event history on any page/post/sidebar widget too. Timeline event history is an advanced WordPress timeline plugin that showcases your life history timeline or your company’s story timeline in a responsive vertical chronological order based on the year and the date of your posts. It is best plugin to create a timeline theme. You can also convert your blog posts into a blog timeline by using this awesome timeline template maker plugin.

== Installation ==

1. Upload the entire `timeline-event-history` folder to the `/wp-content/plugins/` directory.
2. Activate the plugin through the 'Plugins' menu in WordPress.
3.Create gallery and copy its shotcode. 
4. Now paste its shortcode to any page/post/widget.
